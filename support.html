How to Play

  • Set up players — Enter both players' names and ages on the setup screen.
  • Place your ships — Tap a grid square to place each ship. Tap a placed ship to rotate it.
  • Answer to fire — On your turn, answer a reading or math question correctly to earn your shot.
  • Attack — Tap any square on the enemy grid to fire. A hit lights up orange; a miss turns grey.
  • Win — Sink all 5 of your opponent's ships before they sink yours!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Wi-Fi or a data connection?
No. SinkOrThink is fully offline. No internet connection is needed to play.

How many players does it support?
Two players share one device, passing it back and forth each turn.

How are questions chosen?
Questions are selected based on each player's age entered at setup, so kids and adults can play together fairly.

What age is the app appropriate for?
SinkOrThink is rated 4+ and suitable for all ages. Questions scale from beginner to more advanced based on the age entered.

Is there a way to skip a question?
No — you must answer a question to take your turn. This keeps the learning element front and center!

Does the app collect any data?
No personal data is ever collected or shared. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
