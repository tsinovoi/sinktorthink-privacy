Privacy Policy

4+ • Family Friendly • No Ads

Effective date: March 22, 2026

SinkOrThink ("we", "our", or "the app") is developed by Eli Tsinovoi. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use SinkOrThink on your iPhone or iPad.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. SinkOrThink is a local, pass-and-play game. All gameplay — including player names, ages, and game progress — exists only on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

  • No accounts or sign-ins are required
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • No advertising networks are used
  • No data is stored outside your device

Player Names and Ages

Players may optionally enter names and ages to personalize their game session. This information is used only to display on-screen during gameplay and to select age-appropriate questions. It is stored locally on your device and deleted when the game session ends. It is never shared with us or anyone else.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

SinkOrThink is designed for players of all ages, including children under 13. Because we collect no personal data of any kind, the app is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Parents and guardians can allow children to use the app with full confidence that no information about them is collected, stored, or shared.

Internet Access

SinkOrThink does not require an internet connection to play. The app does not make any network requests during normal use.

Third-Party Services

SinkOrThink does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising platforms, analytics tools, or social media frameworks.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no data at risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, or breach.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
